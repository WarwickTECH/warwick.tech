ABOUT the event

What is a Hackathon?

Hackathons are collaborative programming events, where makers and innovators use technology create unique and exciting projects in just 24 hours - a "jam session for makers". Students from all over the EU (and beyond!) come together to make, create and innovate! If you're unsure, check out our blog!

Who are WarwickTECH?

WarwickTECH is a tech society with a focus and aim to bringing together like minded students to build and share their ideas. We're a small team of passionate students who want to connect the makers, creators and innovators on and around campus. We host meetups, workshops, hackathons and more!

What if I can't code?!

We welcome participants with or without programming experience. In fact, we've tried our hardest to ensure that this WarwickHACK is as beginner friendly as we can possibly make it! We'll be running beginners' workshops all throughout the weekend to help you get started.

Where will it be?

WarwickHACK will be held in Warwick Business School at the University of Warwick. Further location details will be provided shortly.

What should I bring?

You should bring your own laptop and any other kit you want to work on across the weekend. While we will be providing a quiet rest area with sleeping mats, you should bring a sleeping bag and/or a pillow for increased comfort.

Code of Conduct

In order to keep our events safe and inclusive for everyone, both our team and all of our attendees must follow to the MLH Code of Conduct. By registering for this event you agree to adhere to the policy outlined.
